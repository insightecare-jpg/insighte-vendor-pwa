# Product Overview

## What is Insighte?
Insighte is a structured **care marketplace** for children's mental health, therapy, education, and developmental support.

## The Core Problem
Parents cannot easily discover, evaluate, or book qualified professionals for their child's specific developmental needs. The market is fragmented — therapists, shadow teachers, special educators, and homeschooling providers all live in silos.

## What We Solve
We create a single, opinionated platform that:
1. Translates a parent's lived problem into a structured care pathway
2. Matches that pathway to verified, tiered providers
3. Enables seamless booking, payment, and ongoing engagement
4. Builds long-term retention through session notes, packages, and progress tracking

## Core Transformation
```
❌ "Find a therapist"
✅ "Solve my child's need through structured care"
```

## System Flow
```
Problem → Service → Provider → Session → Notes → Progress → Package → Retention
```

## Users We Serve
- **Parents** — primary users seeking care for their child
- **Providers** — therapists, educators, shadow teachers, specialists
- **Admins/Ops** — Insighte team managing platform operations

## What Makes It Different
- **Services Engine**: Use-case driven discovery (not just category browsing)
- **Provider Tiers**: Partner / Independent / Royale — first-class differentiation
- **Session Notes**: Public/private separation with clinical-grade privacy
- **Gamification**: Providers are rewarded for quality, not just volume
- **Packages & Credits**: Long-term engagement over one-off transactions
