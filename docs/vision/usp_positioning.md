# USP & Positioning

## Positioning Statement

> **Insighte is not a therapist directory.**  
> It is a structured child development and care system — a marketplace backed by an outcomes engine, tuned for long-term engagement.

---

## Core USPs

### 1. Services Engine (The Differentiator)
Most platforms ask: "What kind of therapist do you want?"  
Insighte asks: "What's your child struggling with?"

Use-case driven discovery means parents find help using their own language, not clinical taxonomy.

### 2. Provider Tiers (Trust Architecture)
Three distinct tiers — Partner, Independent, Royale — with clear badges, quality signals, and ranking implications. Parents instantly understand who they're booking.

### 3. Session Notes (Transparency Layer)
Parents get real visibility into what happens in sessions via public notes. Providers maintain private clinical records. The separation is enforced at the database level.

### 4. Packages & Credits (Retention Engine)
Insighte isn't a one-time discovery tool. Packages commit parents to care journeys. Credit tracking creates natural re-engagement loops.

### 5. Provider Gamification (Quality Loop)
Providers are incentivized to deliver quality, not just volume. Levels, ratings, notes completion, and retention metrics drive provider rankings — which drives their revenue.

## Competitive Landscape

| Platform Type | What They Do | What We Do Better |
|---|---|---|
| Generic directories (Practo, etc.) | List providers | Structured discovery + outcomes |
| WhatsApp communities | Informal referrals | Trust signals + booking infrastructure |
| International platforms (Wyzant, etc.) | Session booking | India-first + child-specific + gamification |
| Hospital OPDs | Appointments | Speed, convenience, continuity |

**Tone**: Warm, direct, empowering — never clinical, never corporate.  
**Feel**: Netflix meets a knowledgeable friend.  
**Promise**: "We make the right care easy to find, book, and keep."
