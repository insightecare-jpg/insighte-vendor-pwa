# User Personas

---

## Persona 1: Priya (Parent)

**Age**: 34 | **Location**: Bengaluru | **Device**: Mobile first

### Background
Priya has a 7-year-old son who has been recently diagnosed with ADHD. She's overwhelmed by the number of providers online, unsure about credentials, and anxious about committing to expensive packages without knowing if the provider is right for her child.

### Goals
- Find a qualified, reviewed ADHD specialist
- Understand what a session will look like before booking
- Track her son's progress over time
- Understand cost upfront — no hidden fees

### Frustrations
- Generic "therapist directories" with no context
- No visibility into what happens in sessions
- Providers who ghost after payment
- Too much clinical jargon

### How Insighte Serves Her
- Use-case search: "my child can't focus" → ADHD Support → Matched providers
- Public session notes → transparency into session outcomes
- Package system → commitment safety net with credit tracking
- Provider badges + ratings → trust signals before booking

## Persona 2: Ananya (Provider — Independent)

**Age**: 29 | **Location**: Remote / Varies | **Specialization**: Special Education

### Background
Ananya is a certified special educator with 4 years of experience. She currently manages her clients via WhatsApp and Google Calendar. She loses bookings because of no-shows and struggles with payment collection.

### Goals
- Reduce admin overhead
- Get discovered by the right parents
- Maintain professional session notes digitally
- Build a sustainable income stream via packages

### Frustrations
- No professional storefront
- Payment chasing
- No structured system for notes
- Clients don't return after initial sessions

### How Insighte Serves Her
- Verified profile with credentials + services displayed
- Booking management + calendar integration
- Session notes system (public + private)
- Provider dashboard with earnings tracking
- Gamification — level up as she completes more quality sessions

## Persona 3: Rahul (Admin / Ops)

**Age**: 27 | **Location**: Bangalore | **Role**: Insighte Operations

Rahul handles provider approvals, dispute resolution, and payout management. Currently everything is done via spreadsheets and WhatsApp.

- Handle refunds and disputes

- Payout management module
- Dispute resolution workflow
